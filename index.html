Who We Are

Welcome to Elite Sports Institute. The home of Elite youth football development, specialised training, international tours and sports specific products. Our mission is to give young aspiring football players the opportunity and platform to professional football training.

We provide clear pathways for players to reach their maximum potential through structured development systems. From beginners to elite level athletes, we support every stage of growth.

Why Do We Do What We Do?

We believe development must start early in a professional environment that builds discipline through football. The game has the power to transform lives far beyond sport.

Football builds teamwork, resilience, respect, and responsibility. More importantly, it builds character through both winning and losing experiences.

What We Offer

We offer structured programs for youth development focusing on technical, tactical, physical, and mental growth.

Players gain access to tournaments, camps, and competitive environments designed to challenge and elevate performance.

We also provide coaching mentorship, elite training systems, and international exposure opportunities for serious athletes.

Meet Our Team

Admire Nyathi

Founder & Technical Director

Admire Nyathi is the Technical Director of Elite Sports Institute. His expertise, experience and dedication to youth football development have established him as a respected specialist in player development and coaching education.

Over the years, he has attained coaching certifications through respected football governing bodies including the South African Football Association (SAFA), Confederation of African Football (CAF) and The Football Association (The FA).

He further expanded his expertise through Coerver Coaching, completing both the Introduction to Coerver Coaching and the prestigious Youth Development Diploma, strengthening his understanding of modern player development methodologies and age-specific coaching practices.

Driven by a commitment to continuous improvement, Admire has completed numerous coaching and development courses through The Football Association while actively engaging with The FA Coach platform to further enhance his technical and tactical knowledge.

Throughout his coaching journey, he has worked across several respected clubs and football programmes in South Africa, including Young Stars FC (Alexandra), Mamelodi Sundowns FC Satellite Programme, CBC Pretoria, Rodadau FC Ladies (Limpopo), Northerns FC (Waterfall, Midrand) and Total Football Excellence (Waterfall, Midrand).

Drawing from these experiences, Admire established Elite Sports Institute in Bulawayo, Zimbabwe, creating an environment dedicated to producing technically exceptional footballers capable of competing at the highest levels of the game.

Elite Sports Institute embodies his football philosophy: developing technically brilliant players who are tactically adaptable, mentally resilient and prepared to perform in the world's most demanding football environments.
